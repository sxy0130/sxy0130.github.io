ALMA

ALMA 未来海洋载具——为死亡而设计

By Zhang & Chen & Gong & Song

2035年，冰川消融，人类重返海洋。人们在蓝海里诞生，也同样在蓝海中死亡，在死后成为特别的存在——ALMA。逝者家人亲自将骨灰装载其中，然后在灵魂殿堂望着它缓缓加入ALMA大家庭。无数的ALMA随着海浪律动，呼吸，构成了一幅奇特的海底亡灵景观。每一只ALMA都托载着一个故去的灵魂，也托载着生者无尽的思念。

In 2035, glaciers will melt and humans will return to the ocean. People are born in the blue ocean, also die in the blue ocean, and become a special existence after death - Alma. The deceased's family personally loaded the ashes, and then watched it slowly join the Alma family in the soul hall. Countless Alma move and breathe with the waves, forming a strange landscape of the dead on the sea floor. Each Alma carries a dead soul and endless thoughts of the living.
