BAMBOUNCE

BAMBOUNCE 弹弹凳

By Song

黑客精神是超越物体的定义，发掘物体的新潜能。我选取了宜家竹制的上菜夹作为凳面单体，通过层叠与重复的方式构造一个兼具弹性与韧性的坐面。而凳腿选取了支架作为支撑结构，连接部分则使用常见的螺栓、螺帽与螺丝杆。人在凳子上体验时既可前后摇摆，也可左右晃动，亦可以在二人坐时创造弹弹的交互。

The spirit of hacking is to go beyond the definition of an object and explore its new potential. I chosen the bamboo serving clip made by IKEA called OSTBIT as the stool surface monomer, and constructed a sitting surface with both elasticity and toughness through the way of overlapping and repeating. The legs of the stool are supported by a bracket called ODDVALD, and the connecting part uses common bolts, nuts and screws. People can swing back and forth, or from side to side when playing on the stool, or create the interaction of bouncing when two people are sitting.
