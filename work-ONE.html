O.N.E 电子产品清洁工具

By Xiao & Song

O.N.E设计的初衷是寻求一体化的工具进行电子产品的清理，以解决电子产品藏匿各类污物难以清理的问题。产品由外壳和内部组成。透明磨砂质感的塑料外壳主要考虑产品的美观性与整洁性。内部主要由喷瓶、清洁刷、硅胶刮板组成，附上裹在结构外的麂皮绒清洁布。功能的转换通过简单拿取盖子等方式完成。

O.N.E is to seek an integrated tool to clean electronic products, so as to solve the problem that it is hard to clean all kinds of dirt hidden in electronic products. The product consists of shell and inner part. The transparent frosted plastic shell mainly considers the beauty and cleanliness of the product. The inner is mainly composed of spray bottle, cleaning brush and silicone scraper, with suede cloth wrapped outside the structure for cleaning use. Function conversion can be completed by simply taking the cover.
