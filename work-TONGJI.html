早安/晚安，同济！

Good morning, Tongji! & Good night, Tongji! 早安/晚安，同济！—— 文创徽章

By Fang & Song

当快节奏的生活与“内卷”时代来临，繁忙与压力占据了大部分人的生活主体。在这样的陀螺式生活中，或许我们缺少的温暖正是一句“早安”和“晚安”。若说“打工人”一词是六分调笑，三分自嘲，再加上一分荒谬；那么“同济人”一词便是六分归属，三分温暖，再加一分梦想。于是“早安，同济人”和“晚安，同济人”便将将这份温存传递给每一位同济师生，让每一个离家的人不再是一个人，而是彼此陪伴的、会对你说一句“早安”“晚安”的同济人。

When the era of fast-paced life and "Involution" comes, busyness and pressure occupy the main body of most people's life. In such a gyroscopic life, perhaps the warmth we lack is a "Good morning" and "Good night". If the word "Migrant Worker" is six points of teasing, three points of self mockery, plus one point of absurdity; Then the word "Tongji" means six points of belonging, three points of warmth and one point of dream. So "Good morning, Tongji" and "Good night, Tongji" will pass this warmth to every Tongji teacher and student, so that everyone who leaves home is no longer a person, but Tongji who accompany each other and will say "Good morning" and "Good night" to you.
